Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 bread slice
  • onion
  • green chilli
  • Coriander leaves chopped
  • Slice of Cheese
  • Ketchup

Step-by-Step Bread Omelette Recipe

  1. Into a bowl, crack open eggs and add in the cumin seeds, garam masala, red onion, bell pepper, red chili powder and add some salt
    Mix well with a fork.

  2. Heat a pan over medium heat and pour in the egg mixture. Cook on medium heat.

  3. Dunk one side of each slice of bread in the egg mixture on the pan.
    Then flip the slide of bread and place face down into the egg mixture.

  4. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the bottom side is cooked. Flip the whole thing
    (omelette along with bread slices).

  5. Place a slice of cheese, some chopped cilantro and fold the hanging flaps of the omelette inwards.
    Fold again so the two slices of bread are on top of each other.
