Kulfi 🍨

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups evaporated milk
  • 1 ¼ cups sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 4 slices white bread, torn into pieces
  • 4 slices white bread, torn into pieces

Step-by-Step kulfi making

  1. Combine evaporated milk, condensed milk,
    and whipped topping in a blender and blend in pieces of bread until smooth.

  2. Pour mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish or two plastic ice cube trays,
    sprinkle with cardamom and freeze for 8 hours or overnight.

Enjoy the kulfi with your girl...😉
